## Framework axes to develop (from design.md)

The ROI framework needs 5 evaluation axes. Candidates based on use case research:

1. **Deployment speed** — weeks to months to quarters
2. **Evidence quality** — operator case study vs. trial vs. vendor claim
3. **Automation level** — open-loop recommendation vs. closed-loop autonomous
4. **Data/integration cost** — new probes needed? proprietary data streams?
5. **Scalability** — site-level vs. network-wide, hardware dependencies
